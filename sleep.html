How Sleep works

Hbit Sleep is designed to feel like Apple Health / Oura: simple entries, smart suggestions, and a calm overview.

  1. Log last night Tap Log night or any day in the calendar to enter when you went to bed, woke up, quality and notes.
  2. Plan your sleep Choose your desired wake time. We suggest bedtimes in 90‑minute cycles and highlight the most gentle option.
  3. Stay consistent Use the weekly schedule to keep a stable routine on weekdays, with a softer variation on weekends.

Your entries sync with the Home overview so your sleep ring and weekly stats stay up to date.
