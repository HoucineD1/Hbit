Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Welcome to Hbit ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application at hbit-d62a6.web.app. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account data — name, email address, and profile information you provide during registration.
  • Usage data — habits, budget entries, sleep logs, and mood logs you voluntarily enter in the app.
  • Technical data — browser type, device type, and general location (country/region) for analytics and security purposes. We do not collect precise GPS coordinates.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the Hbit service.
  • Sync your personal data across sessions via Firebase.
  • Improve app features based on aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.
  • Communicate important service updates or security notices.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Firebase & Google Services

Hbit uses Google Firebase for authentication, real-time database, and cloud Firestore storage. Data is processed and stored on Google servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Firebase Cloud Firestore with authentication-based access controls — only you can read and write your own data. We implement industry-standard security measures, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Export your data at any time from your profile page.
  • Delete your account, which permanently removes all associated data.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

Hbit uses browser localStorage to cache module data for offline performance. No third-party advertising or tracking cookies are set.

7. Children's Privacy

Hbit is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's profile page or by opening an issue on our project repository.
